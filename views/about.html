About us

Ever felt that you just couldn't find your ideal movie day out? From deciding between the ideal timeslots to choosing the right Cinemas at the right prices, there is just much to consider before finalizing your booking!

Cinemax aims to add more flexibility into your search results, intergrating all movies being screen from our cinema partners, for easier convenience of comparing of prices, locations and timings of your ideal movie booking. We also display movies that you may have not heard of but may interest you so you can find out more information about it. You could purchase your tickets directly through Cinemax as the middle man and we will set up all you need before your movie!
